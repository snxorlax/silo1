The Cold Row

A lawless group known for its discretion and strategic edge. The members are unknown, but a large bounty is promised to anyone able to bring in information or associates related to The Cold Row. Over the years they have targeted traveling merchants, and stolen an array of weapons and armor. Most recently they been accused of masterminding the assassination of Sir Mostar Middlehouse and his entire caravan as it trecked through The Dregway.
