Paradigm

The glassy stone was discovered by Sir Acorn Stew, the famous gnomish archivist. It is said to have been the spark which ignited a new Fire of Invention. It was later reverse engineered, and is now easily reproduced. Paradigm was used to rebuild over three dozen cities over 300 years. It is used in larger structures from the Towers of Squall, to the Brilliant Bridges. It is occasionally used for smaller instruments as well, including vehicles and more rarely weapons.
