A Brief History

7735: The Dragons lay siege to the Silos. Known as the First Crush, the mortals held. It lasted 2 years.

7761: The Second Crush was devastation. 10 Silos fell, leaving only Silo IV and VII intact. 90% of gold was stolen. More than 60% of mortals died, and fewer than one thousand Dwarves remained. The landscape was shredded, as the continents were turned to islands. Almost 200 Dragons were killed.

7844: Silos V, XI, XII excavated by Sir Acorn Stew. Paradigm discovered.

7923: Silo II unburied by another team of gnomish archeologists and explorers. Banterok, or Joke Stone discovered.

8033: Silo VI, VIII, IX, X are excavated.

8141: The present year.
